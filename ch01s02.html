Features

There are several distinguishing features of infrastructure programming:

  1. The programs are usually designed to run unattended, at regular intervals, with no human interaction.

  2. The end users are system administrators, or other highly technical engineers.

  3. Programs often rely on querying multiple systems, such as DNS, LDAP, and other databases.

  4. The programmers are typically people who have a background in systems administration but limited experience with programming.
