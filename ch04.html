Parenthesis

Always use parenthesis around method names. Avoid using parenthesis around simple function calls.

Example 4.1. Parenthesis

# Good
puts 'hi' or raise 'bye'

# Bad
puts('hi') or raise('bye')

# Good
File.new('/tmp/foo', 'w')

# Bad
File.new '/tmp/foo', 'w'
